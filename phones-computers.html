Protect against malware

Update OS/apps, avoid unknown downloads, and use trusted security tools.

Protect your Android device

Install from trusted sources, keep Play Protect enabled, and lock the phone with PIN/biometrics.

Protect your iOS device

Use latest iOS, strong device passcode, and review app permissions regularly.

Protect your Windows computer

Enable Defender, firewall, updates, and device encryption where available.

Protect your Mac computer

Enable FileVault, keep macOS updated, and install apps from trusted sources only.

Protect your Linux computer

Use official repos, keep system packages patched, and enable full-disk encryption.
