Core Concepts

Protect against physical threats

Think about device theft, office break-ins, shoulder surfing, and lost phones. Use screen locks, privacy filters, and safe storage.

Create and protect multiple online identities

Separate personal, work, and public identities. Use different emails, usernames, and recovery methods to reduce damage if one identity is exposed.

Protect the privacy of your online communications

Prefer end-to-end encrypted tools, verify contacts, and avoid sharing sensitive details in plain text channels.

Protect devices while traveling

Carry minimum data, keep devices encrypted, disable auto-connect Wi-Fi/Bluetooth, and have a recovery plan before travel.
