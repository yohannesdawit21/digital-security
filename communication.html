Communication

Simple guide

Your communication needs protection

Your communication includes social media, emails, and messaging apps. If not protected, attackers can spy, scam, or steal your data.

Protect Yourself on Social Media

Risks:

  • Fake accounts (impersonation)
  • Oversharing personal information
  • Phishing links in messages

Examples:

  • Someone creates a fake account using your name
  • You post your location and attackers track you
  • A friend sends a hacked link

Oversharing personal information can reveal your location, routines, and private details.

How to stay safe:

Keep your account private
Accept only people you know
Enable 2FA
Think before posting: "Can this be used against me?"

Secure Your Email Communications

Why email is important: It controls many accounts (password reset and login).

Risks:

  • Phishing emails
  • Fake attachments with malware
  • Account takeover
Example: "Your account will be deleted! Click here now!" This is often a fake phishing email.

How to protect:

  • Check sender email carefully
  • Do not open unknown attachments
  • Do not click suspicious links
  • Use strong password and 2FA
  • Log out on shared computers

Use Messaging Apps More Securely

Risks:

  • Account hacking
  • Spyware links
  • Fake messages

Examples:

  • "Click this video" link installs malware
  • Someone uses your account to scam others

How to protect:

  • Enable 2-step verification
  • Do not click unknown links
  • Lock your app with PIN or fingerprint
  • Update apps regularly
  • Verify important messages by calling the person

Recover from Account Compromise

What it means: Your account is hacked or controlled by someone else.

Signs of hacking:

  • Password changed without you
  • Messages sent that you did not send
  • Unknown login alerts

What to do immediately:

  • Change your password
  • Enable 2FA
  • Log out from all devices
  • Check account activity
  • Inform your friends to avoid scams

If you cannot access the account: use Forgot Password and contact app support.
