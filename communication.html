Social Engineering Attacks

Phishing

Fake emails or websites that steal login details.

Vishing

Voice calls pretending to be support, bank, or government.

Smishing

Fraud messages by SMS that include malicious links.

Pretexting

Attacker creates a fake story to make you reveal data.

What to Do

Protect social media data

Limit public profile details, review privacy settings, and enable login alerts.

Secure email communication

Use strong passwords, 2FA, and verify sender domains before replying or downloading attachments.

Use secure messaging

Prefer end-to-end encrypted apps and verify safety numbers/identity when possible.

Recover from compromise

Reset passwords, sign out from all sessions, and review recovery options immediately.
