🛡️ WHAT DO YOU NEED TO PROTECT?

Focus on these five areas first:

Personal information: name, phone, passwords
Devices: phone, laptop, USB drives
Online accounts: email and social apps
Communication: messages, calls, chats
Identity: who you are online

👉 If these are not protected, attackers can hack, spy, or steal your data.
