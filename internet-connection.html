How internet censorship works

Traffic can be blocked by DNS filtering, IP blocking, deep packet inspection, or platform-level restrictions.

Browse more securely

Use HTTPS-only mode, secure DNS when available, and block risky scripts/trackers.

Circumvent blockages and monitoring

Use reputable VPNs, bridge relays, or trusted circumvention tools when legal and safe.

Anonymize connections

For stronger anonymity needs, use Tor Browser and reduce identifying account logins.
